Celkon Q405

Flaunt your personal style statement with the all-new Celkon q405 smartphone that comes with the most engaging features along with an impressive design. This dual SIM smartphone offers multiple connectivity options such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS and more. It packs the latest processor along with an advanced operating system (OS) to facilitate seamless multitasking and smooth operating experience. Stay social 24 x 7, play entertaining games online, download movies and enjoy your nonstop conversation with friends with this excellent Android OS based smartphone brought to you by Celkon. You can buy Celkon q405 smartphone at best price along with 1 year Celkon India warranty only at Snapdeal.

Dual SIM Support

Keep your personal and professional contact numbers in separate SIM cards through the dual SIM enabled smartphone brought to you by Celkon. It also facilitates you to choose the best pricing plans of two different mobile networks simultaneously. 

10.16 cm (4) WVGA Display

Everything looks great on the bright 10.16 cm (4) WVGA display of the Celkon q405 smartphone. Watch your favourite videos and play high quality games on the bright WVGA display of the device that showcases every image with infused clarity and details. It offers 480 x 800 pixels screen resolution for an enhanced viewing experience like never before.

Android 4.4.2 KitKat Operating System (OS)

Power-packed with the latest Android 4.4.2 KitKat Operating System (OS), the Celkon smartphone amazes you with an incredible operating speed. It facilitates you with new and exciting features that would simply sweep you off your feet.  This user-friendly Operating System offers an immersive mode that quickly hides everything else except the app that you are using.  Additionally, it also facilitates you with features such as smarter caller ID, upgraded Hangout App, Ok Google etc.

1.2 GHz Quad Core Processor

Wait less and do more with the new q405 smartphone by Celkon that comes with an advanced 1.2 GHz Quad Core processor. This outstanding processor ensures smooth data transition that allows you to quickly browse web without waiting. Additionally, 512 MB of RAM enhances the processing speed of the phone and amazes you with a seamless multitasking experience.

3.2 MP Primary Camera

Capture the world around you with the 3.2 MP primary camera of the Celkon q405 smartphone. This advanced flash enabled camera enables you to click flawless pictures infused with ultimate clarity and picturesque details.  You can even add special colour effects and get a full screen preview of the clicked image.

1.3 MP Front Camera

Do you love clicking selfies? If yes, then the Celkon smartphone has something exciting for you. It features an excellent 1.3 MP front camera that allows you to click stunning self-portraits for sharing on Facebook, Instagram and other social networking sites.  You can even fine-tune your image through the scene mode. Additionally, it also enables you to stay hooked up with friends via high quality video calls.

Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Connectivity

Download your favourite movies, play entertaining games online, send emails, work on presentations online through the Wi-Fi enabled smartphone brought to you by Celkon.  By using a 3G data card you can enjoy blazing fast internet connectivity on the go. Moreover, the Bluetooth with A2DP connectivity of the phone enables you to transfer data instantly even when there is no internet connection. Additionally, you can quickly find out your location through the GPS connectivity of the phone.

4 GB ROM and 32 GB Expandable Memory

With 4 GB ROM the Celkon q405 smartphone allows you to store your favourite videos, images, files and other important documents in the device without any hassle. Moreover, if you still require some additional space in the phone you can use a microSD card and expand the memory of the phone up to 32 GB.

1500 mAh Battery

Maximize your productivity with the Celkon Campus smartphone that comes with a powerful 1500 mAh battery.  Work on your business projects, play entertaining games online, listen to music and browse web without a pause with the highly advanced smartphone brought to you by Celkon.

FM Radio

From the trending songs to the latest news broadcast, the Celkon Campus q405 smartphone keeps you entertained wherever you go with its FM Radio. Celkon q405 also facilitates you with voice recording feature that enables you to record your voice messages instantly without any hassle.

Swift Keyboard

Type your text messages faster along with enhanced accuracy with the Swift keyboard of the Celkon smartphone. It offers 81 Language Support along with autocorrect feature that facilitates you with a smooth typing experience like never before.

1 Year Celkon India Warranty

Buy Celkon q405 smartphone at best price only at Snapdeal and avail 1 year Celkon India warranty along with your purchase. The warranty protects the product against all manufacturing defects. In case of any problem you may contact the nearest authorized Celkon India service centre to avail a free service within the warranty period.

About Celkon

Celkon Mobiles is a leading mobile manufacturing company in India with its headquarters in Hyderabad. It is the sixth largest mobile handsets company in India and also one of the members of the Android One Project. Its product portfolio includes mobiles and tablet PC’s solution and wireless technologies.
